## 0. How to use this

The model connects the **instrumented** numbers (AI token COGS via `ai_token_usage`; MRR/ARR/ARPU/churn via
`GET /api/billing/saas-metrics`) to the **assumed** numbers (CAC, TAM, infra base, usage intensity). Where a
value is live-instrumented, the source endpoint is named so you can replace the assumption with actuals.

## 2. AI COGS per tenant (the instrumented line)

At the illustrative blended cost of **0.14 THB / 1k tokens**:

| Plan | Avg tokens/day | AI COGS/mo (THB) | Included cap/day | AI COGS at FULL included use (THB/mo) |
|------|----------------|------------------|------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Starter | 0 (AI off) | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| Pro | 60,000 | ~252 | 200,000 | ~840 |
| Enterprise | 600,000 | ~2,520 | 2,000,000 | ~8,400 |

> The **hard ceiling** caps the worst case: a Pro tenant cannot exceed 500k tokens/day (~2,100 THB/mo AI
> COGS) and pays overage (12 THB/1k) on everything above 200k — so heavy use is revenue-positive, not a
> margin leak. This is the structural fix to the "unlimited bucket" the panel flagged.

## 3. Blended COGS & gross margin per tenant

Per-tenant infra allocation = infra base / paying tenants = 52,500 / 50 = **1,050 THB/mo** (illustrative;
falls as tenants grow — infra is largely fixed, so margin improves with scale).

| Plan | Price/mo | AI COGS | Infra alloc | Total COGS | Gross profit | Gross margin |
|------|----------|---------|-------------|------------|--------------|--------------|
| Starter | 990 | 0 | 1,050 | 1,050 | −60 | **−6%** ⚠ |
| Pro | 2,900 | 252 | 1,050 | 1,302 | 1,598 | **55%** |
| Enterprise | 15,000 | 2,520 | 1,050 | 3,570 | 11,430 | **76%** |

> **Read this:** at 50 tenants, Starter is **margin-negative** purely on infra allocation — the model surfaces
> that immediately. Two levers fix it: (a) infra base amortizes as tenants grow (at 200 tenants the per-tenant
> alloc drops to ~260 THB → Starter turns positive), and (b) Starter is a funnel into Pro, not a profit
> center. Blended margin across the mix is **~60%** at 50 tenants and rises with scale toward the ≥75% target.

## 6. The margin guardrail (what the model enforces)

1. **No unlimited AI tier** — every plan has a finite hard ceiling (`ai_tokens_daily_max`).
2. **Overage is priced *and collected*** — tokens above the included cap bill at `ai_overage_rate_thb_per_1k`,
   and the monthly `ai_overage_billing` job appends a Stripe invoice item per tenant (idempotent per
   tenant+month), so a heavy tenant is revenue-positive on AI, not a loss. See
   [`pricing-and-ai-cogs.md`](pricing-and-ai-cogs.md).
3. **Off-Opus tiering** — mechanical AI tasks run on Haiku/Sonnet (regression-tested), keeping blended token
   COGS at the assumed ~0.14 THB/1k rather than ~10× that on Opus.
4. **Watch Starter at low scale** — margin-negative on infra allocation until tenant count amortizes the base;
   treat as a funnel, and revisit its price/positioning if the funnel conversion underperforms.
